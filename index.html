OUR STORY

Mutts of
the metal scene

We're a friend group, not a band. Majority Brazilian, with members from Portugal, Argentina, Germany, Poland, the UK and beyond — all of us washed up in Hamburg, all of us metalheads.

A vira-lata isn't an insult. It's a badge of honour — the underdog, the survivor, the friendliest dog on the street.

We started by meeting at gigs, sharing tickets, splitting Ubers back from Markthalle at 2am. Now we travel as a pack — Wacken, Hellfest, every Tuesday-night thrash show nobody else heard about.

No fee, no application, no gatekeeping. Just: come headbang with us and drink a beer.
